Fairfield Off-Road Racing — Baja SAE

Baja SAE is an intense collegiate design series that mirrors the rigors of professional automotive development. We design, build, and compete in the annual Internation Baja SAE competition where tests include hill climb, suspension and maneuverability course, and a 4 hour endurance race around a mile long track. It demands the complete lifecycle of a product, from initial Solidworks conceptualization and FEA analysis to hands-on fabrication and dynamic testing.

Recognizing that good engineering requires even better resources, I founded the Business Subsystem to professionalize our operations. By managing a $15,000+ annual budget and securing $30,000 in sponsorships, I transformed the team into a financially sustainable organization. My role bridges the gap between the engineering and business side, proving that the most successful engineering projects are those backed by strategic communication, data-driven budgeting, and a strong professional brand.

I'm a core leader of Fairfield University's Baja SAE team, contributing across the full vehicle build cycle — from concept and CAD through fabrication, testing, and competition. My work with the team was featured in a Fairfield University news article.
